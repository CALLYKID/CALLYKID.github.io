### Handstand (wall → freestanding)
1. Wall holds 3×20–40s  
2. Shoulder taps 3×10/side  
3. Freestanding attempts 10–15 min practice

### L-sit (tuck → full)
1. Tuck holds 5×10–20s  
2. One-leg extensions 5×5/leg  
3. Parallel bar full holds 5×5–10s
